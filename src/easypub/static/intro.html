Easy Pub

The free and anonymous online publisher.


Features


  • No account required. You are provided with a unique per-publication secret that can be used to update or delete your publication.
  • No tracking. There are no logs / tracking that can be used to correlate identities to publications.
  • Update and delete supported. You can make modifications to your publication or completely remove it.
  • Fast and scalable. This system has been designed to scale and deliver assets incredibly quickly.
  • Free. The site is completely free to use.
